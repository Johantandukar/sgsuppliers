Our Story

Established Sinced : 28 July, 2005 A.D.
Founder / CEO : Rohan Tandukar

Founded on 28th July 2005 by Rohan Tandukar, Stationery General Suppliers has grown to become a trusted name in the office supplies industry. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide high-quality office materials to organizations of all sizes. Over the years, we have expanded our reach, successfully supplying essential stationery and office supplies to a diverse range of clients, both within and beyond the valley.

We offer a more exhaustive and engaging overview of Stationery General Suppliers by expanding the original text, emphasising the company's history, mission, services, achievements, and dedication to quality and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

At Stationery General Suppliers, our mission is to support the productivity and efficiency of businesses by offering top-notch office supplies. We are committed to delivering exceptional products and services that meet the evolving needs of our customers. Whether it's a startup or a large corporation, we provide tailored solutions to ensure every organization operates smoothly and efficiently.

Our Vision

We are committed to continuing to develop and expand, while consistently prioritising the requirements of our customers. We aim to become the premier supplier of office materials, distinguished by our exceptional service, quality, and reliability.
